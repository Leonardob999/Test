Basic Items

This is for simple Items that not need special Abilities like Diamonds or Sticks.

requiredFeatures

Strength controls the hardness of a Block. For Example Stone has a hardness of 1.5 and dirt 0.5. The Block is unbreakable if strenght is -1.0.

durability

Sets the durability of an Item.

setNoRepair

Defines if the Item is repairable or not.
